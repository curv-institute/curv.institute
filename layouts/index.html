What We Do

CURV Institute develops and validates Representational Science—a framework studying how complex systems encode, transform, and regulate information. We examine representation-level failures that cause instability and inefficiency across critical domains.

Focus Areas

We apply our research across eight key domains:

  • Quantum systems
  • Artificial intelligence
  • CFD/MHD simulation
  • Fusion-relevant plasma dynamics
  • Climate and Earth-system dynamics
  • Power grids and critical infrastructure
  • Biological and medical systems
  • Cyber protection and anomaly detection
